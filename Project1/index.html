Description:

Hearts of Iron 4 is a grand strategy game creaed by Paradox Development Studios based in Stockholm, Sweden. The game was release on June 6, 2016 on PC.

The premise of the game is that you can control any country during the World War 2 time period and command that nations armies, navy, airforce, and economy.

Pick from nations like: The Untied States, Germany, Soviet Union, France, United Kingdom, Italy, Japan, China and guide that nation through the most important conflict in modern history.
